About Me

Who am I?

I'm a photographer from Mechanicsburg PA, and I have been shooting for 5 years now. I absolutely love what I do and hope to make it my full time job soon. I combine modern digital cameras with classic top-of-the-line film lenses for a totally unique look.

What do I do?

I photograph anything and everything. From custom classic car builds to couples' anniversary photos and everything in between. I have done a lot of product photography and it is what I consider myself most skilled at. I also offer vintage and modern Nikon lens repair.

Why I Started Edified Photography

I truly have a passion for photography and have a goal to capture the beauty and craftsmanship in everything, and to further that goal, I intend to make Edified Photography my full time job. My company is fully insured and I work off signed contract.
