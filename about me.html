About Me

Who am I?

I'm a photographer from Mechanicsburg PA, and I have been shooting for 4 years now. I absolutely love what I do and hope to make it my full time job soon. I combine modern digital cameras with classic top-of-the-line film lenses for a totally unique look.

What do I do?

I photograph anything and everything. From custom classic car builds to couples' anniversary photos and everything in between. I also do a lot of product photography and it is what I consider myself most skilled at.
